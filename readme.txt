Automatically converts keywords in your blog posts into affiliate links.

== Description ==

Affiliately helps you better monetize your website by automatically displaying affiliate links on your website's content. This really saves time and takes the hassle out of manually creating affiliate links each time you add new content. Plus, it's FREE and very EASY to use. Just insert your affiliate ID and the plugin will automatically generate the links on your website or blog. See more on how it works at [http://www.affiliate.ly/](http://www.affiliate.ly/)

== Installation ==

Upload the Affiliately plugin to your blog, activate it, then enter your affiliate ID and select the merchant where you're registered as an affiliate.

If you want to see stats like number of affiliate links generated & CTR, go to [http://www.affiliate.ly/](http://www.affiliate.ly/).
